📐 Architectural & Numerical Advances

Batched Contextual Reinforcement (Task-Scaling Law)

arXiv:2604.02322

Novel Finding:

  • Train model to solve N problems simultaneously in shared context
  • As N increases:
    • Per-problem token usage decreases
    • Accuracy degrades gracefully
    • N becomes controllable throughput dimension

Key Results:

  • Demonstrates "free lunch" phenomenon
  • Reduces tokens by 15.8% to 62.6% while maintaining accuracy
  • Models develop emergent self-regulated efficiency
  • Implicit constraints avoid adversarial gradients

Why Interesting:

  • Structural incentives unlock latent high-density reasoning
  • Connects to energy functions research

arXiv:2603.19874 — Kartheek Bondugula

Problem:

Solution:

Results:

  • Especially robust under label noise
